About us

HOW We EVOLVED

When mentors and students of FOSS@Amrita thought of scaling up their presence, starting a company was the best option. Today, we are a group of developers, programmers and designers with 250+ upstream commits in less than 2 years, and still growing up. ThinkFOSS is an outcome of powerfull minds and responsive Computers.

  • Mission - To deliver the best training and technology available
  • Expertise - Training, Solutions and Maintenance of Open Source products
